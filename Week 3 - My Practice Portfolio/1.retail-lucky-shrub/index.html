Overview

Lucky Shrub is a plant nursery that specializes in indoor plants. The nursery primarily operates online and has a small storefront in Austin, Texas with an atelier attached for browsing. Lucky Shrub's selection of plants is known for its high-quality and classic look. The owner, an Austin local, is well known for her plant designs.

Founder

Lucky Shrub was founded by a plant enthusiast who has been collecting plants for over 20 years. She has a passion for plants and loves to share her knowledge with others. She is also a certified plant expert and has been featured in several magazines and blogs.

Our Products
